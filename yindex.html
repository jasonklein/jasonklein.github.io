trellisd

What would be the lovechild of Facebook and Craigslist, trellisd matches posts from users with complementary posts from other, connected users. In other words, trellisd aims to remove all of the, "Does anyone know anyone who...?" posts in your newsfeed.

trellisd, built responsively, is a complete platform: user accounts and sign-in, profiles, messaging, and Facebook integration.

Dev Jobs(?) Tweets

This app was built solely to help myself in mind: having realized that a number of developer jobs are posted on Twitter, I wanted an efficient way to look for the ones of interest to me.

Using Twitter's API, this app finds and filters tweets (from those the app follows and others) that may be related to dev job openings.

Daft Lighting

Inspired by iDaft, Daft Lightning was an exercise to help me better understand Javascript, jQuery and CSS animations. While the apps above are built on Rails, Daft Lighting runs on Sinatra.
